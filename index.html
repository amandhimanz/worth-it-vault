How We Work

From Request to Delivery

Six steps. Zero confusion. Here's exactly how your freight moves through our system.

01

Submit Your Request

Fill the booking form or WhatsApp us. Tell us what, where, and when — we take it from there.

02

Quote & Confirmation

You get a transparent quote within minutes. Approve it, and your shipment is locked into our schedule.

03

Pickup & Loading

Our truck arrives at your location. Professional loading, secure strapping, paperwork done right.

04

Live Tracking

ELD-based GPS tracking gives you real-time location, speed, and ETA — 24/7 on any device.

05

Border & Compliance

Customs pre-cleared. FAST-certified drivers. Every checkpoint, every regulation — handled.

06

Delivery & Confirmation

On-time arrival. Proof of delivery captured. You're notified the moment your freight reaches its destination.
