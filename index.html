Frequently Asked Questions

Answers, before the road

Everything shippers ask before trusting us with their freight.

01 Which provinces and regions do you service?

We run coast-to-coast across all ten provinces, with dedicated lanes connecting Vancouver, Calgary, Winnipeg, Toronto, Montréal and Halifax. Cross-border capacity reaches 48 US states, so a single booking can move freight from a BC warehouse straight through to a customer in Texas without a carrier handoff.

02 Do you handle cross-border shipments into the US?

Yes. Our fleet is FMCSA and CVOR-certified for cross-border movement, with FAST-approved drivers and brokers handling customs paperwork ahead of arrival. Most northbound and southbound crossings clear without delay, and we coordinate documentation so your freight isn't held at the border.

03 What types of freight and trailers do you operate?

Our fleet covers dry van, flatbed and temperature-controlled reefer trailers, supporting full truckload, LTL and time-critical express loads. Whether it's palletized goods, construction materials, or cold-chain product moving between -20°C and +25°C, there's equipment matched to the load.

04 How do I track my shipment in real time?

Every truck runs on ELD-based GPS tracking, giving you live location, ETA and status updates from pickup to delivery. Dispatch shares a tracking link at booking, and our operations team is reachable 24/7 if you need a direct status check on a specific load.

05 What is your average on-time delivery rate?

We close out at 99.7% on-time performance across the network, year over year. That comes from route optimisation, proactive dispatch and a maintenance program that keeps breakdown-related delays rare rather than routine.

06 How quickly can a truck be dispatched for an urgent load?

For time-critical freight, we maintain a dedicated express fleet that can typically be dispatched same-day. Call or submit a booking request with your pickup window and we'll confirm equipment availability and a guaranteed delivery slot within the hour.

07 Are your drivers and vehicles safety certified?

We're ISO 45001 certified with CVOR compliance across the fleet. Every driver completes ongoing safety training and every vehicle goes through scheduled inspection cycles — our incident rate sits 82% below the industry average as a result.

08 How do I get a freight quote or book a shipment?

Reach out through the contact form or call our dispatch line with pickup and delivery details, freight type, and weight. We'll respond with a quote and the earliest available pickup window — usually within the same business day.
